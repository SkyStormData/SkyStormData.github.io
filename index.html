What We Do

Storm Intercept

We chase severe weather across the Ohio Valley, getting up close to supercells, tornadoes, and severe thunderstorms to document and study these powerful systems.

Research Data

Every chase contributes valuable meteorological data — atmospheric soundings, storm structure observations, and environmental parameters that advance severe weather research.

Real-Time Reports

We provide ground-truth reports during active severe weather, helping meteorologists and emergency managers make critical decisions when lives are on the line.

Documentation

High-quality video and photography of severe weather phenomena for research, education, and media — capturing nature's raw power responsibly.
